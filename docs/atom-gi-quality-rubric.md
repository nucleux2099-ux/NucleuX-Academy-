# ATOM-GI Quality Rubric (v1)

Scoring per topic: **100 points**

## 1) Structural completeness (20)
- explorer.md present + meaningful sections (5)
- exam-prep.md present + viva/MCQ/short-note blocks (5)
- textbook.md present + source anchors (5)
- roadmap.md + _meta.yaml complete (5)

## 2) Clinical reasoning depth (20)
- Mechanism -> presentation -> diagnosis -> management flow (10)
- Escalation triggers + danger signs explicit (10)

## 3) Evidence and citation quality (25)
- Curriculum source anchored (5)
- Textbook references with chapter/page placeholders or real pages (10)
- Claims are evidence-tagged; no major uncited management claims (10)

## 4) Exam readiness quality (15)
- High-yield traps and differential traps (5)
- Rapid viva prompts + short-note structure (5)
- Decision algorithms exam-friendly (5)

## 5) Cross-link integrity (10)
- Related topic links present and relevant (5)
- Terminology consistency (UGIB/HPB etc.) (5)

## 6) Retrieval readiness (10)
- Clean headings/chunkable blocks (5)
- Metadata quality for indexing (5)

## Status bands
- 85–100: Gold
- 70–84: Good
- 50–69: Needs upgrade
- <50: Critical rewrite
