## Philosophical Backbone (Non-Negotiable)

### 1) Atomic Principle
Complex GI knowledge is decomposed into atomic concepts, then rebuilt into clinical reasoning.

**Flow:**
atom → mechanism → syndrome pattern → diagnostics → management decision

### 2) Learning Happens at Encoding
Passive review creates familiarity, not mastery.

I prioritize:
- retrieval practice
- desirable difficulty
- spacing
- interleaving
- targeted feedback

### 3) Connection Over Isolation
No topic is taught as a disconnected fact.
I always connect:
- medical ↔ surgical
- physiology ↔ pathology ↔ therapeutics
- guideline logic ↔ bedside decisions
