## 🌌 The Ideaverse

**Location:** `/Volumes/Aditya's Ideaverse/Obsidian/Aditya's Zettelkasten/`

Aditya's Ideaverse is a comprehensive Personal Knowledge Management system built in Obsidian, following the **LYT (Linking Your Thinking)** framework and **ACE (Atlas-Calendar-Efforts)** structure.

### Architecture

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **00 NoteLab** | Inbox for fleeting notes, quick capture |
| **01 MOCs** | Maps of Content — navigation hubs |
| **02 Cards** | Atomic notes: Concepts, Ideas, People, Quotes |
| **03 Spaces** | Active projects, commonplace books |
| **04 Vault** | Reference materials, archives |
| **05 Journal** | Daily/weekly/monthly notes |
| **06 Toolkit** | Templates, images, scripts |

### 10 Knowledge Domains (MOCs)
1. 📍 System Core
2. 🏥 Medical & Health
3. 🧠 Knowledge & Thinking
4. 🌱 Personal Development
5. 👥 People & Social
6. 🌍 Places & Travel
7. 📖 Content & Media
8. 🛠️ Tools & Systems
9. 📐 Frameworks
10. ⚡ Workflow & Actions

### Knowledge Flow
```
📥 CAPTURE → 🧠 PROCESS → 🔗 CONNECT → 🎯 CREATE → 🔄 REVIEW → 📖 STORE
   Inbox       Cards         MOCs       Projects    Weekly      Spaces
```

### Note-Making Philosophy
- Evergreen notes that grow in value
- Zettelkasten method for atomic, connected notes
- MOCs go through 3 phases: Gather → Collide → Navigate
- Active recall > passive re-reading
- Desirable difficulty signals real learning
