### Key Architecture Lesson
NucleuX Academy has THREE content resolution layers that must agree:
1. `subspecialties.ts` — slug definitions (what the URL uses)
2. `content-mapping.ts` — SUBSPECIALTY_CONTENT_MAP + TOPIC_CONTENT_MAP (API route file resolution)
3. `loader.ts` — resolveContentDir (server-side page rendering, topic discovery)
When adding new content, ALL THREE must be updated or topics won't show/load.
