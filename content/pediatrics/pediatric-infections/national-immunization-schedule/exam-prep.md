# National Immunization Schedule (Infections) — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
12 VPDs covered by NIS. India polio-free since 2011. Measles-Rubella campaign largest ever. ROTAVAC = Indian rotavirus vaccine. Key VPD features: Koplik spots (measles), pseudomembrane (diphtheria), whooping cough (pertussis), lockjaw (tetanus), AFP (polio), CRS (rubella). AFP surveillance = polio monitoring. All VPDs notifiable.

## High Yield Points
- ★ **Koplik spots** = pathognomonic of measles (buccal mucosa, day 2-3)
- ★ **Grey pseudomembrane** = diphtheria (bleeds on removal)
- ★ **Whooping cough:** Paroxysmal cough + whoop + post-tussive vomiting; lymphocytosis
- ★ **Neonatal tetanus:** Unable to suck on day 3-14; prevented by maternal TT
- ★ **India polio-free** since January 2011
- ★ **AFP surveillance:** 2 stool samples within 14 days of onset
- ★ **CRS triad:** Cataracts + sensorineural deafness + PDA
- ★ **Measles complications:** Pneumonia (#1 cause of death), SSPE (late)
- ★ **Diphtheria myocarditis** = most common cause of death
- ★ **JE** = Culex mosquito; endemic areas; high mortality

## Mnemonics

### Measles "3 Cs + K + R"
- **C**ough, **C**oryza, **C**onjunctivitis + **K**oplik spots + **R**ash (head→body)

### CRS: "CDS" — Cataracts, Deafness (sensorineural), Septal defects (cardiac)

### NIS 12 Diseases: "TB-DPT-P-H-Hi-Pn-R-M-Ru-JE"
