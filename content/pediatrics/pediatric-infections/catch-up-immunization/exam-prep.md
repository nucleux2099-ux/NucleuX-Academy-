# Catch-Up Immunization — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
Never restart an interrupted schedule — resume from where left off. Every dose counts. Minimum intervals must be maintained. Multiple vaccines same day OK (different sites). DPT not after 7 years (use Td/Tdap). Live-live gap ≥4 weeks. BCG after 5 years: Mantoux first. Mission Indradhanush = India's catch-up drive. No maximum interval between doses.

## High Yield Points
- ★ **Never restart** — resume interrupted schedule; every dose counts
- ★ **Minimum interval** between primary doses: usually 4 weeks
- ★ **DPT maximum age** = 7 years; use Td/Tdap after
- ★ **OPV booster** maximum = 5 years
- ★ **Live-live gap** = ≥4 weeks if not given same day
- ★ **Multiple vaccines same day** = safe; give at different sites
- ★ **BCG after 5 years** = do Mantoux first (IAP)
- ★ **Unknown status** = assume unimmunized; revaccinate
- ★ **HepB minimum intervals:** 4 weeks (1-2), 8 weeks (2-3), 16 weeks (1-3)
- ★ **Mission Indradhanush** (2014) = India's catch-up immunization initiative

## Mnemonics

### **NEVER** Restart — "RESUME and RESPECT Intervals"
- **R**esume from last valid dose
- **E**very dose counts
- **S**imultaneous vaccines OK
- **U**se minimum intervals
- **M**aximum age limits (DPT <7y, OPV booster <5y)
- **E**valuate status using records/recall

## Common MCQ Topics
1. **Interrupted schedule** — Resume, never restart
2. **DPT max age** — 7 years (switch to Td/Tdap)
3. **Live-live interval** — 4 weeks (if not simultaneous)
4. **Multiple vaccines same day** — Safe and recommended
5. **BCG after 5 years** — Mantoux first
6. **HepB intervals** — 4-8-16 weeks minimum
7. **Unknown immunization** — Treat as unimmunized
8. **Mission Indradhanush** — 2014, India
9. **Rotavirus max age** — Brand-specific (generally by 8 months)
10. **Every visit** — Give ALL due vaccines (minimize missed opportunities)
