# Acute Diarrheal Disease & ORS — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
ORS = most important medical advance. Low-osmolarity ORS: 245 mOsm/L, Na 75, glucose 75. SGLT-1 co-transport mechanism. Rotavirus = #1 severe dehydrating diarrhea. Zinc 20 mg × 14 days. Plan A (home), B (ORS 75 mL/kg/4h), C (IV RL). Continue feeding. Antibiotics only for cholera/shigella/amoeba. ReSoMal for SAM (lower Na, higher K).

## High Yield Points
- ★ **SGLT-1 co-transport** = scientific basis of ORS (Na + glucose absorbed together 1:1)
- ★ **Low-osmolarity ORS:** Na 75, glucose 75, osmolarity 245 — reduces stool by 25%
- ★ **Old ORS → new ORS:** Na reduced 90→75, osmolarity 311→245
- ★ **Zinc mechanism:** Restores mucosal integrity, enhances immune function, reduces secretion
- ★ **ReSoMal** = for SAM ONLY; Na 45, K 40 (prevents cardiac failure)
- ★ **Plan C IV fluid** = Ringer's Lactate (preferred); 30+70 mL/kg protocol
- ★ **Rotavirus** = #1 cause of severe dehydrating diarrhea worldwide
- ★ **Rice-water stools** = cholera; cholera toxin → cAMP → secretion
- ★ **Continue breastfeeding** — NEVER starve during diarrhea
- ★ **WASH** = most important long-term preventive strategy
- ★ **Handwashing with soap** = most effective behavioral intervention
