# Developmental Delay — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
Delay >2 SD in developmental milestones. GDD = ≥2 domains in <5 years. Most common cause: genetic (Down syndrome). Screen with DDST-II. Diagnose with Bayley Scales. Early intervention is key. Red flags: no smile 2mo, no head control 4mo, no walk 18mo.

## High Yield Points
- ★ GDD: ≥2 domains delayed, >2 SD below mean, child <5 years
- ★ Intellectual disability: IQ <70, child >5 years; mild (50-70) = 85% of cases
- ★ Down syndrome = most common identifiable cause of ID
- ★ Fragile X = most common inherited cause of ID
- ★ CMV = most common infectious cause of developmental delay
- ★ DDST-II: screening tool, 4 domains, 0-6 years
- ★ Bayley Scales = gold standard diagnostic assessment
- ★ Developmental regression → think neurodegenerative disease (NOT just delay)
- ★ First investigations: hearing test + vision + thyroid function
- ★ Brain plasticity highest in first 3 years → early intervention critical

## Mnemonics
**Red flags — "2-4-9-12-18":**
- **2** months: no social smile
- **4** months: no head control
- **9** months: no sitting
- **12** months: no standing
- **18** months: no walking, no words

**Causes — "TORCH + CAP":**
- **T**ORCH infections
- **C**hromosomal (Down, Fragile X)
- **A**sphyxia (birth)
- **P**KU / metabolic
