# Development Milestones — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
Four domains: gross motor, fine motor, language, social. Cephalocaudal and proximodistal. Social smile 6-8 weeks. Head control 3 months. Sits 8 months. Walks 12-15 months. Pincer grasp 9-12 months. First words 12 months. 2-word sentences by 2 years. Stranger anxiety 8-9 months. Global delay (≥2 domains) needs workup. DDST-II = most widely used screening.

## High Yield Points
- ★ **Social smile** = 6-8 weeks (first social milestone)
- ★ **Head control** = 3 months
- ★ **Sits without support** = 8 months
- ★ **Pincer grasp** = 9-12 months (immature at 8-9, mature at 10-12)
- ★ **Walks independently** = 12-15 months
- ★ **First words** = 12 months; **2-word sentences** = 2 years
- ★ **Stranger anxiety** = 8-9 months (normal cognitive development)
- ★ **Copies circle** = 3 years, **cross** = 4 years, **triangle** = 5 years
- ★ **Tower of cubes:** 2 (12m), 4 (18m), 6-7 (2y), 9-10 (3y)
- ★ **DDST-II** = most widely used developmental screening tool
- ★ **Red flag:** Loss of acquired skills = regression → investigate urgently

## Mnemonics

### Gross Motor: "3-6-9-12"
- **3** months: Head control
- **6** months: Sits with support
- **9** months: Sits without support, crawling
- **12** months: Walks (with/without support)

### Copy Shapes: "Circle-Cross-Square-Triangle" = 3-4-4½-5 years
- **C**ircle = **3** years
- **C**ross (+) = **4** years
- **S**quare = **4½** years
- **T**riangle = **5** years

### Cube Tower: "1-2-3-6-9" (age in years × 3 ≈ cubes)
- 12m = 2, 18m = 3-4, 2y = 6-7, 3y = 9-10
