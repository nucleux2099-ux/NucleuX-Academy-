# Iodine Deficiency Disorders — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
IDD = most common preventable cause of mental retardation. Spectrum: goiter → hypothyroidism → cretinism. Urinary iodine = best community indicator (<100 μg/L = deficient). Two types of cretinism: neurological (deaf-mutism, spastic diplegia) and myxedematous. Prevention: Universal Salt Iodization (≥15 ppm at consumer level). NIDDCP replaced NGOCP in 1992.

## High Yield Points
- ★ **Most common preventable cause of mental retardation** worldwide
- ★ **Urinary iodine excretion** = best indicator of community iodine status
- ★ **Neurological cretinism** = deaf-mutism + spastic diplegia + squint + mental retardation
- ★ **Myxedematous cretinism** = severe hypothyroidism + growth failure + myxedema
- ★ **Universal Salt Iodization** = most cost-effective intervention for IDD
- ★ **Potassium iodate (KIO₃)** used in India (more stable than KI)
- ★ **Salt iodine level:** ≥30 ppm production, ≥15 ppm consumer level
- ★ **NIDDCP** replaced NGOCP in 1992
- ★ **Goitrogens:** Cabbage, cauliflower, cassava (thiocyanates)
- ★ **Jod-Basedow phenomenon** = iodine-induced hyperthyroidism in deficient persons
- ★ **Brain damage in cretinism is irreversible** — prevention is key
- ★ **Neonatal TSH >5 mIU/L** in >3% neonates = community IDD

## Mnemonics

### **CRETIN** — Features of Cretinism
- **C** — Coarse features, cold intolerance
- **R** — Retardation (mental + growth)
- **E** — Enlarged tongue (macroglossia)
- **T** — Thyroid dysfunction (↑TSH, ↓T4)
- **I** — Intellectual disability (IQ <70)
- **N** — Navel hernia (umbilical hernia)

### Neurological vs Myxedematous: "Neuro = Nerves, Myxe = Metabolism"
- Neurological: deaf, spastic, squint (nervous system damage)
- Myxedematous: myxedema, severe growth failure, atrophic thyroid (metabolic)
