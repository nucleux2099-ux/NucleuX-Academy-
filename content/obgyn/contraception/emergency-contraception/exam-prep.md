# Emergency Contraception — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary

Methods to prevent pregnancy after unprotected intercourse. Cu-IUD is most effective. LNG within 72h, UPA within 120h. Works by delaying ovulation — not abortifacient.

## High Yield Points

- ★ Cu-IUD is the MOST effective emergency contraceptive (failure rate <0.1%)
- ★ LNG 1.5 mg single dose — effective within 72 hours (up to 96h)
- ★ UPA 30 mg — effective up to 120 hours; works even after LH surge begins
- ★ Yuzpe regimen: EE 100μg + LNG 500μg × 2 doses 12h apart (largely replaced)
- ★ EC works by delaying ovulation — NOT abortifacient
- ★ LNG-IUS (Mirena) is NOT effective as emergency contraception
- ★ Cu-IUD can be left in for long-term contraception (10 years)
- ★ UPA is a selective progesterone receptor modulator (SPRM)
- ★ No teratogenic effects if EC fails and pregnancy continues
- ★ Avoid hormonal contraception for 5 days after UPA use

## Mnemonics

**EC Timeline: "1-3-5"**
- 1 dose of LNG
- within 3 days (72 hours)
- or UPA/Cu-IUD within 5 days (120 hours)

**Most effective to least effective EC:**
- Cu-IUD > UPA > LNG > Yuzpe
