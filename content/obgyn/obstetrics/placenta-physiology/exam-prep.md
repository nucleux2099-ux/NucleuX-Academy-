# Placenta Physiology — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary

Placenta = chorion frondosum + decidua basalis. Hemochorial: maternal blood bathes fetal villi. Barrier: syncytiotrophoblast + cytotrophoblast + BM + fetal endothelium. Transfer: O₂/CO₂ by diffusion, glucose by facilitated diffusion, amino acids by active transport, IgG by pinocytosis. Hormones: hCG, hPL, progesterone, estrogen. Spiral artery remodeling failure → preeclampsia/FGR.

## High Yield Points ★

- ★ **IgG** is the ONLY immunoglobulin to cross the placenta (by pinocytosis)
- ★ Glucose crosses by **facilitated diffusion** (GLUT transporters)
- ★ Amino acids cross by **active transport** (fetal levels > maternal)
- ★ hPL: **anti-insulin** + lipolytic → diabetogenic effect
- ★ Estriol (E₃) synthesis requires **fetal DHEA-S** (fetoplacental unit)
- ★ Placenta LACKS **17α-hydroxylase** → cannot synthesize estrogen from scratch
- ★ Spiral artery remodeling failure → **preeclampsia + FGR**
- ★ Placenta accreta: no **decidua basalis** between placenta and myometrium
- ★ Placental barrier thins at term: **cytotrophoblast becomes discontinuous**
- ★ Drugs < **500 Da**, lipophilic, non-ionized cross placenta easily
- ★ Normal placenta:fetal weight ratio = **1:6** at term

## Mnemonics

### **"SAFE"** — Transfer Mechanisms
- **S**imple diffusion → O₂, CO₂, drugs
- **A**ctive transport → amino acids, calcium, iron
- **F**acilitated diffusion → glucose
- **E**ndocytosis/pinocytosis → IgG

### **"hCG-hPL-PE"** — Placental Hormones
- **hCG**: maintains corpus luteum, pregnancy test
- **hPL**: anti-insulin, diabetogenic
- **P**rogesterone: maintains pregnancy
- **E**strogen (E₃): needs fetal DHEA-S

### **"AIP"** — Placenta Accreta Spectrum (depth)
- **A**ccreta = Attaches to myometrium
- **I**ncreta = Invades into myometrium
- **P**ercreta = Penetrates through myometrium
