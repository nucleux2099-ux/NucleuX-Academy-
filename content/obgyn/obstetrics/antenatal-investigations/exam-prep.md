# Antenatal Investigations — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary

Booking: blood group, Rh, CBC, HIV, HBsAg, VDRL, urine, TSH. 1st trimester: dating scan + NT + combined test. 2nd trimester: anomaly scan (18-22w), quadruple test, OGTT (24-28w). 3rd trimester: growth scans, GBS (35-37w), CTG, repeat CBC. Rh-negative: ICT + anti-D at 28 weeks + within 72h of delivery. GDM: 75g OGTT, any one value abnormal = GDM (IADPSG).

## High Yield Points ★

- ★ **Booking visit essentials**: blood group, Rh, CBC, HIV, HBsAg, VDRL, urine R/M, TSH
- ★ **Dating scan**: 11-14 weeks, CRL most accurate for GA
- ★ **Anomaly scan**: 18-22 weeks — screens structural malformations
- ★ **OGTT (75g IADPSG)**: Fasting ≥92, 1h ≥180, 2h ≥153 mg/dL — ANY one = GDM
- ★ **Anti-D prophylaxis**: 300 μg IM at 28 weeks + within 72h of delivery if baby Rh+
- ★ **CVS timing**: 11-13 weeks; Amniocentesis: 15-18 weeks
- ★ **CVS miscarriage risk**: 1-2%; Amniocentesis: 0.5-1%
- ★ **GBS screening**: rectovaginal swab at 35-37 weeks
- ★ **Normal Hb in pregnancy**: ≥11 g/dL (WHO)
- ★ **Asymptomatic bacteriuria**: MUST treat (risk of pyelonephritis + preterm labor)

## Mnemonics

### **"BRHUVS-T"** — Booking Investigations
- **B**lood group + Rh
- **R**BC count (CBC/Hb)
- **H**IV + HBsAg
- **U**rine routine
- **V**DRL (syphilis)
- **S**ugar (blood glucose)
- **T**SH (thyroid)

### **"1-2-3 Trimester Scans"**
- **1**st: Dating + NT (11-14 weeks)
- **2**nd: Anomaly (18-22 weeks)
- **3**rd: Growth (28, 32, 36 weeks)

### **"92-180-153"** — IADPSG OGTT Cutoffs
- Fasting: **92**
- 1 hour: **180**
- 2 hour: **153**

## Common MCQ Topics

1. **Most accurate parameter for GA** → CRL at 11-14 weeks
2. **GDM screening timing** → 24-28 weeks (earlier if high risk)
3. **IADPSG criteria** → 75g OGTT, any ONE value abnormal = GDM
4. **Anti-D timing** → 28 weeks (antenatal) + within 72 hours postpartum
5. **ICT positive means** → Mother already sensitized (anti-D antibodies present)
6. **CVS vs amniocentesis** → CVS earlier (11-13w) but higher miscarriage risk
7. **Anomaly scan timing** → 18-22 weeks
8. **GBS prophylaxis** → Intrapartum Penicillin G if culture positive at 35-37 weeks
9. **Mild anemia in pregnancy** → Hb 10-10.9 g/dL
10. **TORCH screening** → NOT routine; only if clinically indicated
