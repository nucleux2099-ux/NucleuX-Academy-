# Antenatal Visit Schedule — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
WHO recommends 8 ANC contacts; Indian national program mandates minimum 4 visits. Booking visit ideally before 12 weeks includes baseline investigations, dating scan, TT, and IFA. Visits increase in frequency as pregnancy progresses.

## High Yield Points
- ★ WHO 2016: minimum 8 contacts during pregnancy
- ★ Indian NRHM: minimum 4 ANC visits
- ★ First visit (booking): before 12 weeks — most comprehensive
- ★ Frequency: monthly → fortnightly (28–36 wk) → weekly (36 wk–term)
- ★ IFA: 100 mg iron + 500 μg folic acid daily from 2nd trimester
- ★ TT: 2 doses, minimum 4 weeks apart
- ★ GDM screening: 24–28 weeks by OGTT
- ★ Anomaly scan: 18–20 weeks
- ★ Calcium supplementation: 1 g/day recommended in low-intake populations
- ★ Periconceptional folic acid: 400 μg/day (prevents NTDs)

## Mnemonics
**Danger signs in pregnancy — "BEHEAD":**
- **B**leeding vaginal
- **H**eadache severe
- **E**dema (face/hands)
- **A**bdominal pain severe
- **D**ecreased fetal movements

**Booking visit investigations — "BRUSH HIV":**
- **B**lood group + Rh
- **R**andom blood sugar
- **U**rine routine
- **S**yphilis (VDRL)
- **H**emoglobin
- **H**BsAg
- **I**V — HIV
- **V**itals (BP, weight)
