# Stages of Labor — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary

4 stages: 1st (onset→full dilatation), 2nd (full dilatation→delivery), 3rd (delivery→placenta), 4th (placenta→2h postpartum). Active phase from 4 cm, ≥1 cm/hr. Partograph: alert line at 1 cm/hr, action line 4h right. AMTSL: oxytocin 10 IU IM + controlled cord traction + uterine massage → reduces PPH 60%. Schultze (80%): fetal surface first. Matthews Duncan (20%): maternal surface first.

## High Yield Points ★

- ★ **Active labor begins at 4 cm** (WHO now says 6 cm for arrest diagnosis)
- ★ **Partograph action line**: 4 hours right of alert line — intervention needed
- ★ **Active phase rate**: ≥1 cm/hour cervical dilatation
- ★ **Fundal dominance**: contractions strongest at fundus → downward propagation
- ★ **AMTSL components**: uterotonic (oxytocin 10 IU IM) + CCT + uterine massage
- ★ **Schultze mechanism** (80%): fetal surface first (shiny), central separation
- ★ **Matthews Duncan** (20%): maternal surface first (dirty Duncan), lateral separation
- ★ **Signs of placental separation**: gush of blood, cord lengthening, uterus rises + globular
- ★ **Prolonged 2nd stage**: >3h with epidural, >2h without (nullipara)
- ★ **3rd stage >30 min**: retained placenta → manual removal
- ★ **True vs false labor**: true = regular, increasing, progressive cervical change

## Mnemonics

### **"DCEL"** — Friedman's Active Phase
- **D**eceleration phase (8-10 cm)
- **C**lear maximum slope (5-8 cm, fastest)
- **E**arly acceleration (4-5 cm)
- **L**atent phase (0-4 cm, before active)

### **"Shiny Schultze, Dirty Duncan"**
- **Schultze**: Shiny fetal surface first (80%)
- **Duncan**: Dirty maternal surface first (20%)

### **"OXYTOCIN"** — AMTSL
- **O**xytocin 10 IU IM (within 1 min of delivery)
- **X**tra cord traction (controlled)
- **Y**ank massage the uterus

### Signs of Separation — **"CUPS"**
- **C**ord lengthening
- **U**terus rises and firms
- **P**lacenta bulges suprapubically
- **S**udden gush of blood

## Common MCQ Topics

1. **Most dangerous stage** → 3rd stage (PPH risk)
2. **Rate of cervical dilatation in active phase** → ≥1 cm/hour
3. **Partograph action line** → 4 hours right of alert line
4. **Drug for AMTSL** → Oxytocin 10 IU IM (1st line)
5. **Schultze vs Duncan** → Schultze = fetal surface, 80%; Duncan = maternal surface, 20%
6. **Prolonged latent phase** → >20 hours nullipara, >14 hours multipara
7. **Arrest of active phase** → No progress ≥4 hours with adequate contractions
8. **Brandt-Andrews maneuver** → Controlled cord traction with counter-pressure on uterus
9. **Retained placenta** → 3rd stage >30 minutes despite AMTSL
10. **4th stage monitoring** → Uterine tone + vitals every 15 min × 2 hours
