# 🔍 Breast Cancer — Explorer Mode

> **The Big Picture:** Most common cancer in women. Early detection saves lives. Treatment is multimodal — surgery + chemotherapy + radiation + hormonal therapy. Know the molecular subtypes.

## 💡 Key Atomic Concepts

### Atom 1: Triple Assessment
> Clinical exam + Imaging (mammography/USG) + Pathology (FNAC/core biopsy). All three must agree. Discordance = needs further workup.

### Atom 2: Sentinel Lymph Node Biopsy
> First node(s) to receive drainage from tumor. If SLNB negative → Axillary dissection avoided. Blue dye + radiotracer used.

### Atom 3: Peau d'Orange
> Skin edema causing orange-peel appearance. Due to lymphatic obstruction by tumor. Sign of locally advanced disease.
