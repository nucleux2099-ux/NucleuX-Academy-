## Learning Roadmap

### Suggested Study Order

1. **Anatomy & Physiology** → Esophageal structure, sphincters
2. **Diagnostic Evaluation** → Manometry, pH studies, endoscopy
3. **GERD & Hiatal Hernia** → Medical and surgical management
4. **Motility Disorders** → Achalasia, diffuse esophageal spasm
5. **Barrett's Esophagus** → Surveillance and management
6. **Esophageal Cancer** → Staging, neoadjuvant, resection
7. **Surgical Techniques** → Fundoplication, esophagectomy
