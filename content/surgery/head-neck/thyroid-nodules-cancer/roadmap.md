## 🎯 Core Learning Objectives

- Apply the TIRADS classification to stratify thyroid nodule risk and guide FNAC
- Interpret Bethesda cytology categories (I-VI) and their implications for management
- Distinguish benign nodules from those requiring surgery based on clinical, radiological, and cytological features

## 🔗 Cross-Subject Integration Points

Thyroid nodule assessment is a model of systematic diagnostic workup — integrating clinical examination, radiology (USG TIRADS), pathology (FNAC Bethesda), and molecular testing. The decision-making algorithm from nodule detection to surgical planning exemplifies evidence-based surgical practice.
