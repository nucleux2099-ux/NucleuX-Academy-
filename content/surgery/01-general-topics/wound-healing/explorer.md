## рҹ’Ў Key Atomic Concepts

### Atom 1: Macrophages Are Essential
> Wound healing can proceed without neutrophils (they just clean). But without macrophages, healing STOPS. They orchestrate everything вҖ” debridement, cytokine release, fibroblast recruitment.

### Atom 2: Collagen Type Transition
> Early wound: Type III collagen (weak, thin fibers). Mature wound: Type I collagen (strong, thick fibers). Ratio changes from 3:1 to 1:3 during remodeling. This is why strength increases over months.

### Atom 3: Oxygen Is Critical
> Collagen synthesis requires oxygen (hydroxylation of proline/lysine). Hypoxia вҶ’ poor collagen вҶ’ poor healing. This explains why diabetics (microangiopathy) and smokers (vasoconstriction) heal poorly.
