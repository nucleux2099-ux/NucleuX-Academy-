# Colorectal Cancer

> **Source**: Shackelford Ch 88-91, NCCN Guidelines
> **Topic System**: [[Colon Index]]
> **Phase**: Shoot

---

## Core Concepts

> [!summary] One-liner
> CRC follows the adenoma-carcinoma sequence (CIN 85%, MSI 15%), staged by AJCC 8th edition (T=depth, N=node count with ≥12 nodes required), treated with segmental resection + adjuvant chemo (stage III), and rectal cancer requires MRI staging and TME with possible neoadjuvant CRT.

---

## Epidemiology

- **Incidence:** 3rd most common cancer in US
- **Mortality:** 3rd leading cause of cancer death
- **5-year survival:** 65% overall
- **Colon:Rectal ratio:** ~2:1

---

## Carcinogenesis Pathways

| Pathway | Frequency | Key Features |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| **CIN** (Chromosomal Instability) | 85% | APC → KRAS → SMAD4 → p53 |
| **MSI** (Microsatellite Instability) | 15% | MMR gene defects, Lynch syndrome |
| **CIMP** (CpG Island Methylator) | 32% | Serrated pathway, BRAF mutation |

> 💡 **MSI-H tumors:** Better prognosis, respond to immunotherapy

---

## TNM Staging (AJCC 8th Edition)

### T Classification
| Stage | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| Tis | Carcinoma in situ (intramucosal) |
| T1 | Submucosa |
| T2 | Muscularis propria |
| T3 | Through MP into pericolorectal tissues |
| T4a | Penetrates visceral peritoneum |
| T4b | Invades adjacent organs |

### N Classification
| Stage | Nodes |
|-------|-------|
| N0 | 0 |
| N1a | 1 |
| N1b | 2-3 |
| N1c | Tumor deposits without nodes |
| N2a | 4-6 |
| N2b | ≥7 |

> **Minimum lymph nodes:** ≥12 for adequate staging

## Colon Cancer - Surgical Procedures

| Location | Procedure | Vessels Ligated |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Cecum/Ascending | Right hemicolectomy | Ileocolic, right colic |
| Hepatic flexure | Extended right hemi | + Right branch MCA |
| Transverse | Transverse colectomy | Middle colic |
| Splenic flexure | Extended left hemi | + Left branch MCA |
| Descending | Left hemicolectomy | Left colic |
| Sigmoid | Sigmoid colectomy | Sigmoid vessels, IMA |

### Oncologic Principles
1. **Margins:** ≥5 cm proximal and distal
2. **Complete mesocolic excision (CME)**
3. **High vascular ligation** at vessel origin
4. **Lymph node harvest:** ≥12 nodes

## Adjuvant Chemotherapy

### Stage II (High Risk)
**High-risk features:**
- T4 tumor
- Poorly differentiated
- Lymphovascular invasion (LVI)
- Perineural invasion (PNI)
- <12 lymph nodes examined
- Obstruction or perforation
- Positive margins

**Regimen:** CAPOX × 3 months OR FOLFOX × 6 months

### Stage III

| Risk | Regimen | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| Low (T1-3 N1) | CAPOX | 3 months |
| High (T4 or N2) | CAPOX/FOLFOX | 6 months |

> 💡 **IDEA trial:** 3 months non-inferior for low-risk with CAPOX

## Rectal Cancer

### Anatomic Definitions
- **Upper rectum:** 10-15 cm from anal verge
- **Mid rectum:** 5-10 cm from anal verge
- **Low rectum:** 0-5 cm from anal verge

### MRI Staging (Critical Features)
| Feature | Significance |
|---------|--------------|
| **mrT stage** | Depth of invasion |
| **mrN stage** | Nodal involvement |
| **CRM** | Circumferential resection margin |
| **EMVI** | Extramural vascular invasion |
| **MRF involvement** | Mesorectal fascia |

### Total Mesorectal Excision (TME)
- **Standard of care** for rectal cancer
- Removes rectum with intact mesorectal envelope
- Sharp dissection along mesorectal fascia
- Quality = complete mesorectum, smooth surface

---

## Rectal Cancer Treatment

| Stage | Treatment |
|-------|-----------|
| **T1-2 N0 (favorable)** | TME alone |
| **T3 N0 or T1-2 N+** | Neoadjuvant CRT → TME → Adjuvant chemo |
| **T4 or threatened CRM** | Long-course CRT → TME |
| **Total neoadjuvant (TNT)** | Chemo → CRT → Surgery (increasingly used) |

---

## Key Clinical Pearls

> 💎 **Pearl 1:** Adenoma-carcinoma sequence: APC → KRAS → SMAD4 → p53

> 💎 **Pearl 2:** ≥12 lymph nodes required for adequate staging

> 💎 **Pearl 3:** MSI-H tumors have better prognosis and respond to immunotherapy

> 💎 **Pearl 4:** Rectal cancer needs MRI for staging (CRM, EMVI critical)

> 💎 **Pearl 5:** TME is standard for rectal cancer – quality predicts outcomes

> 💎 **Pearl 6:** IDEA trial: 3 months CAPOX non-inferior for low-risk stage III
