# 📋 Cholelithiasis & Cholecystitis — Exam Prep Mode

---

## 🎯 One-Liners

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Most common type of gallstone | Cholesterol stones (75-80%) |
| Risk factors for cholesterol stones | 4F: Fat, Female, Forty, Fertile |
| Pathognomonic sign of acute cholecystitis | Murphy's sign |
| Gold standard investigation for gallstones | Ultrasound abdomen |
| Treatment of choice for symptomatic gallstones | Laparoscopic cholecystectomy |
| Charcot's triad indicates | Cholangitis (Fever + Jaundice + RUQ pain) |
| Reynolds' pentad adds | Confusion + Hypotension (to Charcot's) |
| Most common CBD stone type | Secondary (migrated from gallbladder) |
| Courvoisier's law | Palpable, non-tender gallbladder with jaundice = NOT stones |

---

## 📝 Clinical Features Comparison

| Feature | Biliary Colic | Acute Cholecystitis | Cholangitis |
|---------|---------------|---------------------|-------------|
| Pain duration | 30 min-6 hrs | >6 hrs, persistent | Variable |
| Fever | No | Yes | Yes (high, spiking) |
| Jaundice | No | Mild/No | Yes |
| Murphy's sign | Negative | Positive | Variable |
| WBC | Normal | Elevated | Elevated |
| Cause | Temporary obstruction | Persistent obstruction | CBD stone + infection |

---

## 🔬 Investigations

| Investigation | Finding |
|---------------|---------|
| **USG abdomen** | Stones, GB wall thickness >3mm, pericholecystic fluid |
| **LFT** | Elevated ALP, GGT if CBD involvement |
| **MRCP** | CBD stones, anatomy |
| **ERCP** | Therapeutic - stone extraction |
| **HIDA scan** | Non-filling GB = acute cholecystitis |

---

## ⚠️ Complications of Gallstones

- Acute cholecystitis
- Chronic cholecystitis
- Mucocele, Empyema, Gangrene
- Perforation → Biliary peritonitis
- Cholangitis
- Gallstone ileus
- Mirizzi syndrome
- Gallbladder carcinoma (porcelain GB)

---

## 💊 Management

```
BILIARY COLIC → Analgesia + Elective lap chole
ACUTE CHOLECYSTITIS → Antibiotics + Early lap chole (<72 hrs)
CBD STONES → ERCP + Lap chole
CHOLANGITIS → Antibiotics + Urgent ERCP/drainage
```
