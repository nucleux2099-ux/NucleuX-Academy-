# Acute Cholecystitis — Exam Prep

## 60-second viva frame
1. Definition + syndrome/stage line
2. Top differential hierarchy
3. Investigation order and why
4. First-line and definitive management
5. Escalation trigger + fallback pathway

## High-yield one-liners
- Exam-safe definition
- Most dangerous miss
- Best first investigation by scenario
- Procedure timing principle
- Follow-up recurrence-prevention line

## Must-know tables to fill
- Etiology and risk profile
- Severity/stage criteria
- Investigation ladder
- Intervention choices by scenario
- Complications and rescue strategies

## Traps to avoid
- Delaying source control while chasing complete diagnostics.
- Using therapeutic procedures as universal diagnostic first step.
- Ignoring physiology severity while deciding intervention timing.
- Missing overlap with adjacent HPB topics.

## Short-note prompts
- Pathophysiology summary
- Unstable patient algorithm
- Role of endoscopy/radiology/surgery
- Long-term prevention and follow-up

## Rapid recall drill
- Q: emergency first move?
- Q: threshold for urgent decompression/surgery?
- Q: when is MDT mandatory immediately?
