# Liver Pathology — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
Cirrhosis = end-stage fibrosis + regenerative nodules. HCC = AFP marker, arises in cirrhosis, aflatoxin B1 → p53. Wilson = ↓ceruloplasmin, copper deposits, KF rings, ATP7B. Hemochromatosis = HFE C282Y, iron overload, "bronze diabetes," Prussian blue+.

## High Yield Points
- ★ **Hepatic stellate (Ito) cells** → myofibroblasts → collagen I/III = key in fibrogenesis
- ★ **Aflatoxin B1** → p53 mutation codon 249 → HCC (Aspergillus on stored grains)
- ★ **AFP** = tumor marker for HCC (not specific; also ↑ in yolk sac tumor, pregnancy)
- ★ **Kayser-Fleischer rings** = copper in Descemet's membrane of cornea (Wilson)
- ★ **"Bronze diabetes"** = skin pigmentation + DM + cirrhosis (hemochromatosis)
- ★ **HFE gene C282Y mutation** = hereditary hemochromatosis (AR)
- ★ **Prussian blue stain** = iron (hemochromatosis); **Rhodanine stain** = copper (Wilson)
- ★ **Fibrolamellar HCC** = young, no cirrhosis, no AFP elevation, better prognosis
- ★ **Portal hypertension** → 4 portosystemic shunts (esophageal, rectal, paraumbilical, retroperitoneal)
- ★ **Asterixis (liver flap)** = hepatic encephalopathy sign

## Mnemonics

- **H**epatorenal syndrome
- **A**scites
- **V**ariceal bleeding
- **E**ncephalopathy
- **J**aundice
- **A**lbumin ↓ (hypoalbuminemia)
- **S**BP (spontaneous bacterial peritonitis)

**Wilson vs Hemochromatosis:**
- **W**ilson = **C**opper = **C**eruloplasmin ↓ = Chromosome 1**3**
- **H**emochromatosis = **I**ron = **H**FE = Chromosome **6**
