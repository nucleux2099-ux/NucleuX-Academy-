# Bleeding Disorders — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary
Platelet/vascular = petechiae, mucosal, ↑BT. Coagulation = hemarthrosis, deep, ↑PT/aPTT. vWD = most common inherited. Hemophilia A = Factor VIII, X-linked. DIC = consumption coagulopathy. TTP = ADAMTS13 deficiency, do NOT transfuse platelets.

## High Yield Points
- ★ **vWD** = most common inherited bleeding disorder; AD; DDAVP for treatment
- ★ **Hemophilia A** = Factor VIII; **B** = Factor IX; both X-linked recessive; ↑aPTT, normal PT, normal BT
- ★ **DIC:** ↑PT, ↑aPTT, ↓platelets, ↓fibrinogen, ↑D-dimer, schistocytes
- ★ **TTP pentad:** Thrombocytopenia, MAHA, neuro, renal, fever; NEVER give platelets
- ★ **ITP:** ↓Platelets, ↑megakaryocytes in marrow, normal spleen size
- ★ **Petechiae** = platelet disorder; **Hemarthrosis** = coagulation factor deficiency
- ★ **Mixing study:** Corrects = factor deficiency; doesn't correct = inhibitor
- ★ **vWF** bridges platelets (GP Ib) to subendothelium AND carries Factor VIII
- ★ **Schistocytes** = DIC, TTP, HUS (microangiopathic hemolysis)
- ★ **Waterhouse-Friderichsen** = bilateral adrenal hemorrhage in meningococcal DIC

## Mnemonics
**PT vs aPTT pathway:**
- **PT** = extrinsic = Factor VII = warfarin
- **aPTT** = intrinsic = VIII, IX, XI, XII = heparin, hemophilia

**TTP pentad: "FAT RN"**
- **F**ever, **A**nemia (MAHA), **T**hrombocytopenia, **R**enal, **N**eurological
