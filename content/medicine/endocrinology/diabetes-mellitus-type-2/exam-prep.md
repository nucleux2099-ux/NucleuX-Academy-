# Diabetes Mellitus Type 2 — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary

90-95% of all DM. Insulin resistance + β-cell failure. Obesity-linked. Metformin first-line. SGLT2i/GLP-1RA for CV/renal protection. May present with complications at diagnosis. UKPDS: tight control ↓ microvascular.

## High Yield Points ★

- ★ **Metformin** = first-line; ↓ hepatic glucose output; contraindicated if eGFR <30
- ★ **SGLT2 inhibitors** = CV and renal protection; cause glucosuria → UTI, genital infections
- ★ **GLP-1 RA** = weight loss + CV benefit; GI side effects
- ★ **Sulfonylureas** cause **hypoglycemia and weight gain**
- ★ **Ominous octet** (DeFronzo) = 8 pathophysiological defects
- ★ **Acanthosis nigricans** = marker of insulin resistance
- ★ **UKPDS** = tight control ↓ microvascular; metformin ↓ CV in overweight
- ★ **EMPA-REG** = empagliflozin ↓ CV mortality (landmark trial)
- ★ **30% have retinopathy at diagnosis** (insidious onset)
- ★ **HbA1c target** = <7% generally; individualize for elderly/comorbid
- ★ **Euglycemic DKA** = SGLT2 inhibitor complication (normal glucose + ketoacidosis)

## Mnemonics

- **Metformin side effects: "GI-LAB"** — GI upset, Lactic Acidosis, B12 deficiency
- **SGLT2i: "FLOSS"** — Fournier gangrene, Low BP, Osmotic diuresis, Sugar in urine, Skeletal (fractures)
- **Ominous Octet**: "My Liver And Pancreas Both Give Kids Sugar" — Muscle, Liver, Adipose, Pancreas (β), Pancreas (α), Brain, GI, Kidney

## Differential Diagnosis

| Condition | Differentiating Feature |
|---|---|
| **T1DM** | Young, lean, autoantibodies, low C-peptide, DKA |
| **LADA** | Adult, lean, GAD+, eventually needs insulin |
| **MODY** | AD inheritance, young, mild, C-peptide present |
| **Cushing syndrome** | Moon face, striae, buffalo hump, elevated cortisol |
| **Acromegaly** | Coarsened features, elevated IGF-1/GH |
| **Hemochromatosis** | Bronze diabetes, iron overload, liver disease |
