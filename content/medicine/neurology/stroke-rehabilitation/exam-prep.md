# Stroke Rehabilitation — Exam Prep

## Quick Summary

MDT-based rehabilitation starting within 24-48 hours. Stroke unit care reduces mortality (NNT=18). Maximum recovery in first 3-6 months (neuroplasticity). Key complications: spasticity, depression, shoulder pain, dysphagia. mRS and Barthel Index for outcomes.

## High Yield Points ★

- ★ **Stroke unit care** reduces mortality and dependency — NNT = 18
- ★ **Early mobilization** within 24-48 hours (if stable)
- ★ **Maximum recovery** in first 3 months; continues up to 1-2 years
- ★ **Post-stroke depression** affects 30-50% → SSRIs first-line
- ★ **Dysphagia screening** before oral intake (risk of aspiration pneumonia)
- ★ **Spasticity** management: physiotherapy → botulinum toxin → baclofen
- ★ **CIMT** (constraint-induced movement therapy) = restraining good limb
- ★ **Modified Rankin Scale** = standard outcome measure (0-6)
- ★ **Shoulder subluxation** = common; prevent with positioning and sling

## Mnemonics
