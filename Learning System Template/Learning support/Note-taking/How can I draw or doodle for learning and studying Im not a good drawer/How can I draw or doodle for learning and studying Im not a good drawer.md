**Note-taking**

Drawing to learn

Many students are put off drawing because they feel that they just aren't artistic. However, drawing skills can be trained, just like almost every other skill. Luckily, you don't need to be an artist, you just need to be able to draw enough to activate the visual processing, artistic expression and abstraction part of your brain.
