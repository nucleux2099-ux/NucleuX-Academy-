## Task management and prioritisation

Asana, ClickUp, or Amazing marvin
 

Amazing Marvin is extremely powerful and probably the most customisable and fully featured task management app on the market. However, it can be overwhelming for beginners.

For most people who want something simpler, Asana has a free plan that is excellent for task management and will meet most people's needs. ClickUp is an alternative to Asana and has a different workflow and user interface. Many people prefer ClickUp to Asana due to its more intuitive workflows, especially when working in teams.
