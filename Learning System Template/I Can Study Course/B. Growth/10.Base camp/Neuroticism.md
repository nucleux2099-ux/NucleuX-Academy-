# Neuroticism
## About neuroticism

Neuroticism is often associated with a fixed mindset and fear of failure. Neuroticism is considered one of the five core personality traits according to the evidence-based [Big Five personality framework](https://www.verywellmind.com/the-big-five-personality-dimensions-2795422).

**Take an evidence-based personality quiz** [**here**](https://bigfive-test.com/)**.**

### Neuroticism can be good

Some neuroticism is beneficial as it keeps you safe, creates high standards, and provides motivation. As a result, neuroticism is common among high achievers. However, excessive neuroticism causes significant mental health problems such as anxiety and depression. This may also explain why anxiety and depression are especially prominent in high-performing individuals.

In the sublessons below, we will introduce some frameworks for managing neuroticism through five stages: prevention, planning, execution, measurement, and management.
