# Hipshot demonstration -  Accounting
View this sped-up version of a 1.5-hour hipshot demonstration performed on VCE Unit 1 and 2 Accounting (Victorian government curriculum in Australia).

_This was initially live-streamed on the iCanStudy Students YouTube channel_ [_here_](https://www.youtube.com/@icanstudystudent)_._

## Watch for...

Watch out for differences in Justin's technique compared to yours. How is his approach to this topic different from what you are used to? What about how he chunks and creates relationships? Or how he uses the resources and Google-driven inquiry?

If the pace or level of the techniques in this video is beyond your current level, don’t be discouraged. You will get there with persistence and marginal gains (and in a fraction of the time it took Justin to!)
