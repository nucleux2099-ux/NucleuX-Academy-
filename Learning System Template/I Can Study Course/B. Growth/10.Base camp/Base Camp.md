# Base Camp

> [!info] Stage focus
> Consolidate advanced encoding with upgraded self-management. Base Camp strengthens Hipshot scheduling, habit systems, and emotional regulation so you can sustain high output.

## How to work this stage

- Implement the Hipshot variation that fits your pace (standard/accelerated/ultra).
- Audit habits (Priority 0+1, PEER system) and run Kolb reflections on adjustments.
- Track neuroticism management strategies to maintain focus under stress.

### Practice checklist
- Hipshot schedule drafted and trialled for at least one week
- Habit system tracked with Kolb reflections (Priority/PEER)
- Neuroticism management plan documented (triggers + interventions)

### Ready to move on when…
1. Hipshot cadence keeps you ahead of syllabus without burnout.
2. Habit routines (Priority 0+1, PEER) are operating and logged.
3. Final checkpoint indicates emotional regulation strategies are in place.
