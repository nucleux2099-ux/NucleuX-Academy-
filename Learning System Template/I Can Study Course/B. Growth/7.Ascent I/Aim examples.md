# Aim examples
In the sublessons below, we demonstrate the usage of the Aim step across a variety of subjects. Even if your particular discipline is not included as an example, the _methods of thinking_ are completely transferrable.

You can skip sub-lessons you are not interested in by marking them as complete. However, we recommend watching several different examples to get a clear understanding of the technique.

**We recommend watching the following sublessons for all learners:** 

1. _**Demonstration of core techniques**_
2. _**Chunking for medicine**_

Both of these sublessons contain highly transferrable learning points for all subjects and disciplines.

## Observe the process

Focus on the processes and approach to thinking about information rather than the actual example contents themselves.
