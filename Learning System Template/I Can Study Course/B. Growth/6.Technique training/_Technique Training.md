# _Technique Training

> [!info] Stage focus
> Retrain how you encode. Technique Training introduces inquiry-based learning (TLS), order control, unlearning, and scheduling upgrades that prime the Bear Hunter System.

## How to work this stage

- Practice TLS daily on live material—expect confusion, that’s desirable difficulty.
- Use Kolb reflections to fine-tune questioning, chunking, and note structure.
- Apply order control to existing study plans before moving forward.

### Practice checklist

- Order-control audit performed on your current study schedule/roadmap
- Final checkpoint submitted with evidence (notes, reflections, schedule)

### Ready to move on when…
1. TLS outputs show clear questioning, relational notes, and chunking revisions.
2. You can explain your chosen learning order for an upcoming topic.
3. Old habits identified in Unlearning are replaced with documented alternatives.
