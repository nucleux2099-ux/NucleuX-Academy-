# Scheduling tips
## Scheduling guidelines

This lesson will show you how to schedule according to your priorities. You can go through this like a checklist.

### Set your priorities first, then set your schedule.

You should transition from setting **weekly** priorities to setting **daily** priorities. Every night, make sure to set your schedule for the next day.

### Guidelines

1. Schedule your top two priority items first.
2. Use either a diary or digital schedule. For most people, digital apps are more efficient and manageable, but if you have a low volume of tasks, pen-and-paper may be sufficient.
3. Be very specific in your scheduling entries. To avoid procrastination, break down your study time into 30-minute blocks, and make sure to leave breaks and gaps.
4. Overestimate the time required to complete each task by 25 to 50%.
5. Ensure 15 minutes of buffer between each task.
6. Have at least one hour of unscheduled time per day as emergency.
7. Schedule time for reflecting and setting priorities at the end of every day.
