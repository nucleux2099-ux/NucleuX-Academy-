## Order control insecurities

# Safety in layers

The [Swiss cheese model](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0925753520300576) shows that it’s extremely difficult to miss information because you are revisiting and asking questions in several rounds. By comparison, if you use conventional linear learning, it is actually much easier to miss things as our brain is not a photocopy machine and will miss many points.
