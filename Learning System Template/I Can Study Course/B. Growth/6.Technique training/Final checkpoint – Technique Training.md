# Final checkpoint – Technique Training
## Checkpoint items

To move on, you must have around 80% competence with the skills taught so far. Use the list below to check whether you're ready!

_**Note:** Lower levels of competence with even one of the items can be problematic during later stages where we build on these foundations._

### Technique training checkpoint

1. You use non-linear, processed notes. You are are becoming comfortable with non-linear mapping.
2. You have a consistent, regular and high-quality Kolb’s cycle.
3. Your inquiry step helps you to explore and identify relationships.
4. You identify multiple relationships from a single concept, not just one or two.
5. You compare relationships to find similarities and differences.
6. You create groups based on these similarities and differences.
7. Your mindmaps have a clear backbone of major ideas.
8. You are seeing progress through marginal gains as a habit.
9. Your spacing schedule is more refined and consistent.
10. Your mastery of interleaving techniques is improving.
11. You do systematic prioritisation and task management as part of your routine.
12. You have reduced distractions and procrastination.

### Unlocking the next stage

Ascent 1 is unlocked 14 days after Briefing is completed. If you've been practising the new theory and progressing at the recommended pace, it should be unlocked already by now!

**If the next stage is still not unlocked, please use this time to practise your current method and gain deeper mastery.**

**Warning:** In the Ascent stages, we have found that any weaknesses in fundamentals will be magnified. In some cases, it can take _months_ to diagnose and repair problems caused by inaccurate fundamentals.
