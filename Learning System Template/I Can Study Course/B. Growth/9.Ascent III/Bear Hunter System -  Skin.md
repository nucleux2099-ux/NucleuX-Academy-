# Bear Hunter System -  Skin
Skin is a step for exclusively refining your higher-order learning at the evaluate level.

As you have probably noticed by now, your chunk structure changes to reflect your most up-to-date understanding of how concepts are related to each other. It can take multiple Aim-Shoot cycles to get to a point where we now have a comprehensive view of all the relationships.

With the Skin step, we consolidate, integrate, refine, and make judgments on how we want our final chunk structure to look. **After all this time, it's finally time to create some more certainty!**
