### Growth phase progress

**Ascent 3 marks the end of the intensive encoding strategies segment of the Growth phase.** 

In the later stages, we start to develop the usage of these skills more time-efficiently and in more complex combinations and systems. Issues with encoding _quality_, _effectiveness_, or _consistency_ are unlikely to be improved by the lessons in future stages. 

If you are doubtful of your competence at this point, the greatest improvement will come from practice and feedback rather than later theory.

## Rapid skill assessment

**If you do not notice the following general improvements from a rapid skill assessment, there is a high chance that your techniques or system requires optimisation.** 

### Expected findings at this stage...

1. Significant improvements with retention after first encoding session.
2. Significant improvements with retrieval performance across a diverse range of challenges and contexts.
3. Reduction in total study time and increase in efficiency due to reduced time spent on relearning.
4. Significant improvements in confidence.
5. Significant improvements in growth mindset and ability to learn from mistakes and experiments.
6. A clear appreciation for the type of thinking that higher-order learning triggers and a high tolerance for cognitive load and discomfort.

If your experience does not align with the above expectations, we **strongly** recommend seeking feedback on your practice.
