# Ascent III — Skin

> [!info] Stage focus
> Refine and consolidate the structure built in Aim/Shoot. Skin challenges you to optimise chunking, build GRINDE maps, and finalise the knowledge schema.

## How to work this stage

- Treat Skin sessions as refinement passes—remove redundancy, emphasise directionality, and capture nuance.
- Apply intuitive chunking and the 2–4 rule to simplify without losing meaning.
- Use example dissections to benchmark map quality across subjects.

### Practice checklist
- At least two Skin passes performed on previous Shoot outputs (before/after saved)
- GRINDE checklist applied to one map and adjustments logged
- Ascent 3 checkpoint quiz completed with noted follow-ups

### Ready to move on when…
1. Your final maps explicitly show grouping, relationships, direction, and emphasis.
2. You can explain simplification decisions (what moved, merged, or was removed).
3. Checkpoint feedback shows no major gaps in Aim/Shoot/Skin linkage.
