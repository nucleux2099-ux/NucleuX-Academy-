# WPW example dissection -  Economics
Key principles

- Make sure you are using reverse explanations instead of terminology crutching.
- Do as much as possible from memory. You want to test your retrieval, not your short-term memory or ability to read a mind map.

_Terminology crutching_ is the most common mistake learners make. It makes the WPW process significantly easier, but by removing desirable difficulty.

## Tip

WPW is a great technique to use for your end-of-week revision. It’s both broad and detailed, covering a vast scope of information and multiple forms of interleaving. It helps you to target your weaknesses quickly and efficiently.
