# Camp II — Advanced Retrieval

> [!info] Stage focus
> Push retrieval to the limits with Whole–Part–Whole drills, interleaving heavy loads, and decision/attention management under exam conditions.

## How to work this stage

- Schedule WPW sessions weekly, using provided worksheets to structure runs.
- Train attention management and task switching with deliberate practice blocks.
- Capture revision pitfalls and fix them via Kolb reflections.
