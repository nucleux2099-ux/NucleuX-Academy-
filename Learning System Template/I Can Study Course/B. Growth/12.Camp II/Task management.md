## Task management

# In addition to the Priority 0+1 system...

Use the following methods to enhance your task management ability.

1. 2-minute rule: Straight-away complete tasks that you can finish within 2 minutes.
2. Eat the Frog: First do the tasks that you don’t want to do.
3. Manage dependencies: Clear bottlenecks by identifying tasks that future tasks depend on. This often involves developing skills or working with others. The task may not appear to be a high priority at first but is important in enabling future tasks.
