# Preparing for revision
We have covered the four layers of learning and the tools to learn each layer properly (including GRINDE maps, flashcards, and the modified method of loci).

The only thing left is a **strong revision strategy** to fill any gaps.

Before revising, you should quickly sweep through your course material to see if you’ve missed anything you need to learn. You can also use the modified method of loci to improve your mind map by replacing lists of information with drawings.
