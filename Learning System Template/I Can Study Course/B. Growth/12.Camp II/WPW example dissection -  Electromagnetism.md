# WPW example dissection -  Electromagnetism
Key principles

- Stay concise. Lack of conciseness can be due to a lack of clarity on how to communicate the importance of something. This leads to over-explanation and difficulties in smoothly transitioning to more detailed parts.
- Do not teach to the pen. The pace of teaching should not be limited by the pace of the mindmap/drawing. This is a common mistake when first using this method.

Note that most of these mistakes are common in those first learning the WPW method, even with high levels of knowledge expertise.
