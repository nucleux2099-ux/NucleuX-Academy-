# Effective revision
## Expanding-gap spaced repetition

Ensure that the gaps between your revision sessions are getting longer. Do at least one or two retrieval practice sessions in the first 10 days and continuously expand the gaps for all subsequent sessions.

## Interleaving

It is inefficient to always practice under the same conditions. The brain learns much better by practising with slight variations each time.

## Gap-seeking

Actively look for gaps. To do this, apply the Aim step again to the study material and your GRINDE map. You will see things in your GRINDE map that you don’t know well and can deliberately ask questions to explore your gaps in a more targeted way.
