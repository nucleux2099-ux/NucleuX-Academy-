# Camp I — Precision Memorisation

> [!info] Stage focus
> When concepts are mastered but arbitrary detail remains, Camp I equips you with targeted memorisation systems (flashcards, loci, WPW) that respect higher-order structures.

## How to work this stage

- Maintain encoding quality from Ascent/ Base Camp—Camp I supplements, not replaces, deeper learning.
- Use Whole–Part–Whole (WPW) to ensure memorisation slots back into logic and concepts.
- Iterate on flashcard design and review strategy (avoid mindless drills).

### Lesson map

- Memorisation frameworks: [[Rote learning and memorisation]], [[Flashcards]], [[Active flashcard management]], [[Modified method of loci]]

### Practice checklist
- WPW plan built for a current topic (document whole/parts/whole)
- Flashcard deck created with active management rules (suspension, tagging, bury)
- Camp I check completed with supporting evidence

### Ready to move on when…
1. Memorisation sessions begin and end with WPW framing.
2. Flashcard metrics show active management (buries, edits, retirements).
3. Camp I check indicates precision memorisation is in place without harming encoding.
