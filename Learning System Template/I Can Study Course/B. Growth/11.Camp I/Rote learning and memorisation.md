# Rote learning and memorisation
We will now learn how to memorise the outer layer of information (i.e., arbitrary information) using high-efficiency memorisation techniques.

Only use rote-learning techniques and memorisation as a _last resort_. These techniques only apply to the outer layer of information – focus on the three inner layers to maximise your conceptual understanding first!
