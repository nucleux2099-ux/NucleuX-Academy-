# Camp I check
## General competence assessment

As the techniques in this stage do not require much (if any) cognitive retraining, you should be able to progress through this stage relatively quickly. Use the table below to check your general competence level, and feel free to move onto Camp II as soon as you feel ready.

|**Technique(s)**|**Level of Competency**|
|---|---|
|Flashcard usage|You can manage your flashcards actively, accurately and sustainably.<br><br>Your flashcards fill rote-learning gaps at layers 3 and 4 of learning.|
|Modified method of loci|You can seamlessly integrate these into GRINDE maps and mind maps while studying for layers 3 and 4 of learning.|
|Focus training and PEER-Peer system|These are a fixed part of your daily routine.<br><br>You may have also noticed some improvements in your natural ability to focus.|
