# Phase structure - Briefing
By now, your foundations are strong, and there shouldn’t be any gaping weaknesses with your study methods. Now, let’s elevate them to the next level!

**The** _**Growth Phase**_ **is broken down into 5 parts.**
## 1. Basic skills and "fitness"

We’ll start off by addressing some essential foundational techniques to refine your system. We’ll also ensure you have the cognitive fitness to ascend smoothly in the later stages.

## 2. First climb

Using your new skills may seem daunting and difficult at first, and it may seem like you are not progressing. We'll give you tasks and tools that help you to self-diagnose and adapt your learning effectively (even without us)!
## 3. Base camp

We’ll start adding higher-level skills and mindsets that build on everything you've mastered so far. Most people choose to spend months at Base camp to let themselves acclimatise to their new systems.

## 4. Push to summit

If the first 3 stages are done correctly, this is the easiest part of the program! On the other hand, people who rush through will find these techniques either impossibly difficult or completely useless (as they don't even realise they are doing it incorrectly)!

## 5. Summit

Reach whatever learning goal you want. By this point, you've been equipped with the tools and trained your brain to think, learn and adapt to any learning challenge. Summit is the level of true confidence based on a track record of consistent results and refined processes.


_A special exam is unlocked upon completing Summit. This exam must be passed to apply for the iCanStudy coach training program (for those interested in pursuing a career in learning coaching)._
