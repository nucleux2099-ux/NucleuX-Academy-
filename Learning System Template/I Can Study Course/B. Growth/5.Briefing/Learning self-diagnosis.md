# Learning self-diagnosis
## Putting it together

Some learners find it difficult to schedule all their studying sessions throughout the week.

Therefore, we've created some example schedules that you can use for typical school or lecture timetables. These schedules have been tried and tested across thousands of students and are safe templates for you to base your own schedules around.

![[Image 18.jpg|4 learning schedule templates on a desk]]

Each schedule includes a **prestudy** and **revision** routine and is rated on:

- **Development speed:** How quickly you would be able to learn the skills in this program
- **Flexibility:** How easily you can adapt to changes in your schedule
- **Time efficiency:** How effectively the schedule maximises available time to produce meaningful work
- **Difficulty:** The complexity of trying to follow the schedule
