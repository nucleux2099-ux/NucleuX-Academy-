# Expectations
## Skills vs knowledge

This program teaches you learning **skills**.

Unlike knowledge (which can be acquired very quickly), skills often take much more time and experience. This is especially true of learning skills which have often become habits. The time to untrain old habits and train new habits can be significant.

### About the Growth phase

Compared to the High-Yield phase, a characteristic feature of the Growth phase is that it involves cognitive habit retraining. This is the primary reason progress is much more _variable_ and generally _slower_ than the progress you may have felt during the High-Yield phase.

## The efficiency/effectiveness balance

There are no instant fixes, but with the proper work and patience, the results will come. Learning is not something you “just do” – it is a skill that must be learned and practised over time to be perfected.

**However, time is limited. Therefore, we have to invest our time in things that:**

1. Are effectiveness (helps us achieve our goals)
2. Are efficient (low time, energy, resource cost)

Many learners have methods that are only efficient _**or**_ effective.

## Dare to be different

To make change, we need to change things!

You will find throughout this program that your methods become different from many people around you. However, we encourage you to think with a critical lens. Ask yourself whether the methods we teach you seem valid and logical. Experience the effects yourself and come to your own judgment.

**Common does not mean correct.**
