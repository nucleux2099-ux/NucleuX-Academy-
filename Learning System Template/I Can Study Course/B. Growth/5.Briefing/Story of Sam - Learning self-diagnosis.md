# Story of Sam - Learning self-diagnosis
## Can you relate to Sam's story?

Hearing other people’s situations, whether similar or different, often helps in our own personal situations. Although you may wish to transform your skills immediately, remember that some things cannot be rushed (otherwise, everyone would do it)!

## Our philosophy

People often underestimate how deep and complex learning skills can be because they are rarely exposed to real learning science. The process of trying to improve is also confusing and sometimes frustrating! Over the years, we've refined our program to make it easier and more achievable than it has ever been. But it still doesn't make the journey easy.

**We've realised that difficulty is an unavoidable part of the growth.** Rather than try to remove discomfort, which may be impossible, we focus on teaching you the skills and mindsets to embrace discomfort and thrive. 

### True empowerment is more than feeling comfortable

We believe this is _true_ empowerment: the ability to not only succeed when it is easy but to face challenges and overcome uncertainties when most others cannot.
