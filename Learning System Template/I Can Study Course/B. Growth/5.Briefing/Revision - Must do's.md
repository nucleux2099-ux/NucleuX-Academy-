# Revision - Must do's
## Recap

We've covered these principles before. This is an opportunity to check your system and ensure you're continuing to do your interleaved retrieval sessions.

**The most basic principles of effective revision are to:**

1. **Schedule your revision**: Have one weekly revision and one monthly revision. This is a very basic form of a learning strategy known as **spaced repetition**.
2. **Use full recall**: Before you open your notes or textbook, try to recall the information first, using various methods such as writing, drawing, mindmaps, teaching someone else, or asking and answering practice questions.
