# Tips and tricks trap
## Learning to learn in the 21st Century

Social media algorithms promote tips and tricks that can be understood in 30 seconds. Unfortunately, social media algorithms do not care about helping you become a better learner. Falling into a trap of tips and tricks can create the perfect recipe for long-term failure.

### Thinking critically about "tips"

Tips and tricks are easy and popular. So, if they worked well, shouldn't everyone be a great learner?
