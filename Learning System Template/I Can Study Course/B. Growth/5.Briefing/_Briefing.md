# _Briefing

> [!info] Stage focus
> Align expectations for the Growth phase. Briefing consolidates high-yield skills, introduces Kolb’s experiential cycle, and sets the criteria for self-diagnosis.

## How to work this stage

- Revisit each high-yield technique and confirm it is in active use (calendar, SIR, VPReFRE, marginal gains).
- Learn Kolb’s cycle and apply it immediately to your most recent practice block.
- Complete the learning self-diagnosis to identify priority dimensions before advancing.

### Practice checklist
- One Kolb cycle completed on a recent study session
- Learning self-diagnosis submitted with noted dimensions to improve
- "Must do" checklist ticked with evidence (calendar, SIR log, VPReFRE examples)

### Ready to move on when…
1. You can summarise your current strengths/gaps using the diagnosis report.
2. Kolb reflections are scheduled (daily/weekly) and you’ve captured at least one.
3. High-yield routines from Fundamentals remain active and logged.
