# Shoot examples
In the sublessons below, we demonstrate the usage of the Shoot step across a variety of subjects. Even if your particular discipline is not included as an example, the _methods of thinking_ are completely transferrable.

You can skip sub-lessons you are not interested in by marking them as complete. However, we recommend watching several different examples to get a clear understanding of the technique.

## Aim-Shoot cycling

Once you have proficiency with Aim and Shoot as individual steps, you can (and maybe already have without any instruction) start transitioning between Aim and Shoot seamlessly in a continuous cycle. Watch the video below to see this in action. You will see the results of this process in each of the subject-specific examples.

### Struggling with Aim-Shoot cycling?

Don't worry. Keep practising, and you should naturally feel like you can do it. Aim-Shoot cycling is often something that develops as a result of upper conscious competence in each individual step.
