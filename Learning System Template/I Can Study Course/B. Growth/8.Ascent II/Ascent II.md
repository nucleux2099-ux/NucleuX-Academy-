# Ascent II — Shoot

> [!info] Stage focus
> Translate Aim scaffolds into rich, non-linear representations. Shoot emphasises VPReFRE note standards, layers of learning, and chunk refinement while you fill the conceptual web.

## How to work this stage

- Enter sessions with Aim questions ready; Shoot is about populating and restructuring answers.
- Enforce the VPReFRE checklist on every set of notes until it’s habitual.
- Use examples and dissections to calibrate quality (compare against your own outputs).

### Practice checklist
- Three Shoot sessions captured with VPReFRE checklist applied
- One example comparison (pick a provided example, annotate differences)
- Layers of learning quiz completed and reflected upon

### Ready to move on when…
1. Your notes meet VPReFRE criteria without the checklist beside you.
2. You can articulate the four layers for your current topic and justify the structure.
3. Shoot sessions feel purposeful—less copying, more transforming and linking.
