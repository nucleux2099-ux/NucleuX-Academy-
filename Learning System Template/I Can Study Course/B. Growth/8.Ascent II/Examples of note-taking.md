# Examples of note-taking
Good note-taking will satisfy VPReFRE – Visual, Processed, Relational, Freehand, Reflective and Efficient. If your mind map is done correctly, it will satisfy this checklist.

We will look at a variety of examples and show you why the notes are good or very bad. **You might be shocked to see that common (even typically applauded) note-taking methods are very ineffective for learning.** 

We will also show you how traditional notes can be improved so you can retain, recall and apply more information.
