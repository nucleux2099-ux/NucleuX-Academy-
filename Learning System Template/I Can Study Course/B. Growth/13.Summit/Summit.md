# Summit — Performance Optimisation

> [!info] Stage focus
> Translate mastery into peak performance. Summit sharpens multipass cramming, exam execution, group strategy, and recovery routines so you can deliver under pressure.

## How to work this stage

- Only enter Summit once BHS, Hipshot, and advanced retrieval are stable.
- Prototype multipass plans on upcoming assessments—iterate using Kolb reflections.
- Stress-test routines (sleep, diet, mindset) in mock conditions.

### Lesson map

### Practice checklist
- Multipass schedule drafted and tested on at least one mock/exam
- Exam execution routine rehearsed (timing, checkpoints, review loops)
- Recovery plan documented (sleep, diet, post-exam debriefs)

### Ready to close the loop when…
1. Multipass runs deliver consistent recall without exhaustion.
2. Exam reflections show minimal unforced errors (“silly mistakes” addressed).
3. Support routines (sleep, diet, group strategy) are systematised and logged.
