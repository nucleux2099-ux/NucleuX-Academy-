# _Fundamentals 2

> [!info] Stage focus
> Install the high-yield toolkit: exam execution, in-class capture, VPReFRE note-making, and scheduling discipline. Mastery here powers most curricula without needing advanced techniques.

## How to work this stage

- Tackle one lesson at a time and run the recommended tasks before moving on.
- Capture VPReFRE note audits weekly to normalise non-linear processing.
- Use exam and class techniques immediately in live contexts (lectures, tutorials, mock exams).

### Lesson map
- [[Exam technique]] — performance pre/during/post exam routines
- [[In-class technique]] — live capture and processing while knowledge is delivered
- [[Note-taking]] — VPReFRE standard and non-linear structures
- [[Study scheduling]] — macro-level planning beyond the 30-day sprint
- [[Revision]] — practical interleaved retrieval strategies
- [[Rote-learning]] — when and how to use memorisation responsibly
- [[Demonstration of core techniques]] — worked examples to model outputs
- [[Fundamentals checkpoint]] — verify competence before entering Growth

### Practice checklist
- VPReFRE checklist completed on at least one set of notes weekly
- One live application each: exam rehearsal, in-class technique, revision block
- Fundamentals 2 checkpoint submitted with evidence of practice logs

### Ready to move on when…
1. You can show VPReFRE-compliant notes and explain your chunking choices.
2. A personal exam routine (before/during/after) is documented.
3. Study schedule balances encoding, retrieval, and review without overbooking.
