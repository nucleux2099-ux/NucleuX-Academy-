# Recommended tasks - Fundamentals - SIR
## Completing these tasks

As spaced, interleaved retrieval requires a spacing interval between retrieval sessions, it can take months to master interleaving techniques.

To complete these tasks, we recommend incorporating SIR into your schedule and having at least one week's worth of practice before moving on. You can continue to practice this over time instead of waiting months to master it before moving to your next lesson.

**Less than one week of practice is not advised.**
