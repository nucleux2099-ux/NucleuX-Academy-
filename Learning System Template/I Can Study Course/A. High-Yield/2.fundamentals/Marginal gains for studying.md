# Marginal gains for studying
In this video, deepen your understanding of marginal gains and see how we can pick compounding instead of additive gains to give us the greatest effect, using school or university subjects/papers as examples.

_This sublesson is aimed at students. Although it may not be as directly relevant for professional learners you can still get some helpful ideas from this example!_
