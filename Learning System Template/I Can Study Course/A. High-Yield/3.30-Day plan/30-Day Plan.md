# 30-Day Plan

> [!info] Stage focus
> Convert momentum into a concrete operating plan. In 30–60 minutes you’ll map outcomes, performance metrics, protected time, and habit scripts for the coming month.

## How to work this stage

- Open the planning form ([[Introduction - 30-Day Plan]]) and work through lessons in order.
- Align every outcome goal with a performance goal and scheduled practice block.
- Revisit the plan weekly—this stage is reusable whenever priorities shift.

### Lesson map
- [[Introduction - 30-Day Plan]] — planning principles vs. intentions
- [[Goal setting]] — write outcome + performance goals the program can support
- [[Performance goals]] — translate desired grades/results into controllable behaviours
- [[Protecting time]] — guard critical practice windows
- [[Limiting habits and scripted actions]] — design habit scripts to remove friction
- [[Environmental optimisation]] — shape the workspace to cue execution
- [[Pace adjustments]] — calibrate workload against energy and commitments

### Practice checklist
- Completed 30-day planner with outcome, performance, and identity goals
- Calendar updated with protected practice blocks and review slots
- Habit scripts and environmental tweaks logged for the next 30 days

### Ready to move on when…
1. The plan is scheduled (not just written) and synced with your calendar.
2. You’ve identified at least two limiting habits plus replacement scripts.
3. Checkpoint review scheduled (weekly or fortnightly) to adjust the plan.
