# Protecting time

## Protecting time

Key principles

- Protect your time by avoiding high-risk blocks and preventing anticipated risks from hijacking your time.
- If you can't protect a block of time, do not schedule important things there.

# Instructions

1. Schedule the important tasks first.
2. Identify factors that put your scheduled tasks at risk.
3. Take steps to protect those tasks. If you can't protect a task, move it to a safer time.
4. Repeat this process for lower-priority tasks.
