## 🚀 Hit the next 30 days running!  
This stage will guide you through planning techniques that optimise your chances of success.

---

# 🌟 **Overview and Recommendations**

- This stage is focused on **planning** rather than practice.  
- Move to the next stage as soon as you complete your plan.  
- Follow the instructions carefully for optimal results.

# 🎓 **Lessons**

## 1️⃣ **Introduction**
- Covers basic principles for **goal-setting** and **planning**.

## 2️⃣ **Goal Setting**
- Understand how **effective goals** can set you up for success.

## 3️⃣ **Performance Goals**
- Learn how to create process-driven goals for **early feedback** and adjustments.

## 4️⃣ **Protecting Time**
- Strategies to safeguard your most **valuable asset**—time.

## 5️⃣ **Limiting Habits and Scripted Actions**
- Identify habits that sabotage progress and replace them with **low-willpower actions**.

## 6️⃣ **Environmental Optimisation**
- Enhance productivity by **removing distractions** and **adding triggers** for good habits.

## 7️⃣ **Pace Adjustments**
- Fine-tune your plan to suit your **personal learning style**.

# 📖 **Introduction**

## 📋 **Accessing Your 30-Day Planner**
- Use our **guided planning form** as you progress through each lesson.
- Revisit and reuse the planner as needed for future planning cycles.

### 🔑 **Key Principles**
- **Goals require plans**: Intention without planning won’t lead to success.  
- **SMART goals** are a good start but need fine-tuning for sustained success.

---

# 🎯 **Goal Setting**

## 🧭 **Key Principles**
- Balance **short-term sacrifices** with long-term success.  
- Hard work is not enough—**direction** and **methodology** matter.  
- Focus on **specific** and **measurable** goals.

### ⚡ **SMART Goals**
- Specific  
- Measurable  
- Achievable  
- Relevant  
- Time-bound

---

# 🏔️ **Performance Goals**

## 🧠 **Key Principles**
- Focus on **processes** and **attributes** like:
  - **Time management**
  - **Learning efficiency**
  - **Stress management**
- Avoid overloading yourself; choose a few goals to start with.

### 🚨 **Beware of Bias**
> "This doesn’t apply to me" or "I’m different" are common pitfalls, especially for high achievers.  
> Stay open-minded to avoid unnecessary mistakes.

---

# 🕒 **Protecting Time**

## 🔒 **Key Principles**
- **Safeguard time blocks** for high-priority tasks.  
- Identify risks to your schedule and protect important tasks first.

### 📝 **Instructions**
1. Schedule important tasks first.  
2. Anticipate risks and take preventive actions.  
3. Move unprotected tasks to safer time slots.  
4. Repeat for lower-priority tasks.

---

# 🔄 **Limiting Habits and Scripted Actions**

## 🛑 **Main Points**
- Identify habits that derail progress.  
- Pay attention to how you respond to **stress**, **fatigue**, and **discomfort**.

### ⚙️ **Scripted Actions**
- Pre-planned responses requiring minimal willpower.
- Use these to replace limiting habits.

---

# 🌟 **Environmental Optimisation**

## 🧹 **Key Principles**
- **Environment dictates behavior**: Remove distractions and bad triggers.
- Make **good behaviors convenient** and **bad behaviors harder** to engage in.

---

# ⏱️ **Pace Adjustments**

## 📅 **Fine-Tuning Your Plan**
- Adjust schedules to fit your **learning capacity**.  
- Submit your plan after creating a balanced schedule.

### 📖 **Practice vs. Theory Time**
- Maintain a **5:1 ratio** of practice to theory.  
  - Example: 25 hours of study = maximum 5 hours of theory.

# 🧩 **Recommended Tasks**

## ✅ **Complete Your 30-Day Plan**
- Use the **guided planning form** to outline your schedule.  
- Save your plan and add it to your calendar.

- Learners who plan deliberately face fewer obstacles and improve more smoothly.
