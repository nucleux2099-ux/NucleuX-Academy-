# Rapid time management
~ [[_Rapid Start]]

> [!important]  Core concepts
>
> 
> 1. Urgency trapping
> 2. Task management vs. time management
> 3. Human biological tendencies for prioritisation and how it harms us
> 4. Urgency vs importance
> 5. The life-changing value of prioritising “importance” instead of “urgent”
> 6. Theory time vs. practice time

We'll explore these concepts in more depth later in the program. You can read our article [here](https://learning.icanstudy.com/learning-support/recommended-apps) on recommended apps for time management, task management and note-taking. You can also find this article again anytime from the [Learning Support](https://learning.icanstudy.com/learning-support) page on the left-side menu.

> [!Leaf] Key principle
> ##### 
> 
> Always practice **often**, **early**, and with increasing **challenges**. Learning and time management are skills. And skills are about what you can _do,_ not what you _know_. One of the most common reasons people struggle to acquire new skills is when there is too much theory and insufficient practice
